Namur, a.k.a. N3R: a Notation3 Reasoner

(well, a very incomplete, quick and dirty one, at the moment)

One important limitation of the current parser is that it requires a whitespace after all prefixed names. So the following is not recognized: 

:a :b :c, :d.
Instead, it must be written: 
:a :b :c , :d .
